Quick overview of virtual meeting

Virtual meetings are real-time interactions that take place over the Internet using

integrated audio and video, chat tools, and application sharing. They offer a way to
engage students in fully interactive, online learning experiences such as lectures,
discussions, and tutoring. Many virtual meeting applications integrate with course
management systems, providing students and faculty with a unified learning system
including access to online meetings.
